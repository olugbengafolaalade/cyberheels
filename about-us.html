About Us

At CyberHeels, we are passionate about revolutionizing the intersection of cybersecurity and wearable technology. With a team of dedicated experts boasting years of experience in cybersecurity, IoT, and fashion, we are committed to empowering individuals to navigate the digital world securely and confidently.

Get Started

CyberHeels introduces a groundbreaking feature allowing users to securely share their wearable's location with trusted contacts through blockchain technology. This feature grants wearers peace of mind in various scenarios, as friends and family can receive real-time updates on their whereabouts, facilitating quick responses in emergencies. With privacy protection measures in place, users maintain control over who can access their location data, ensuring safety without compromising personal boundaries.

How we use blockchain in fashion

Unique Digital Identities

Blockchain identities for each fashion item can be stored securely on the blockchain and associated with specific product details such as brand, design, materials, and manufacturing information which can be tracked throughout its lifecycle

Ownership and Transfer

It  allows for the tracking of fashion items in the secondary market, such as resale platforms or vintage stores. Each time a product is listed for sale or auctioned off, the transaction is recorded on the blockchain, preserving its ownership history and provenance

Sharing Wearable Location

Users can securely share their wearable's location with trusted contacts through blockchain technology. This feature grants wearers peace of mind in various scenarios, as friends and family can receive real-time updates on their whereabouts, facilitating quick responses in emergencies.

Anti-Counterfeiting

Anti-counterfeiting measures such as tamper-evident packaging and smart tags. These tags are equipped with unique identifiers linked to the blockchain, making it difficult for counterfeiters to replicate or tamper with genuine products.
