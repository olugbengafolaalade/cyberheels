About CyberHeels

At CyberHeels, we leverage cutting-edge technology to provide robust solutions for digital and physical security. Our platform utilizes blockchain technology to ensure the authenticity of fashion items and IoT-enabled wearables to enhance personal safety.

Users can securely share their wearable's location with trusted contacts through blockchain technology. This feature grants wearers peace of mind in various scenarios, as friends and family can receive real-time updates on their whereabouts, facilitating quick responses in emergencies.

Our Technology
